What is APP 0?

APP (pronounced APP-zero) stands for Project-Based Learning, which is the innovative teaching approach adopted by ESPRIT in its engineering program. During the course of one week, first-year students have the opportunity to familiarize themselves with ESPRIT's values and prepare for the active pedagogical approach which includes group work, brainstorming, project creation, and real-world scenarios.

This approach is designed to not only enhance students' problem-solving skills and theoretical knowledge but also encourage them to become active participants in their education. By engaging in hands-on projects, students develop essential skills such as critical thinking, collaboration, communication, and creativity, which are highly valued by employers.

Overall, APP 0 provides an immersive and collaborative learning experience that prepares students to become innovative and adaptable problem solvers in their future careers.
